## **✅ Stay Safe Online -- Checklist for College Students (India)** {#stay-safe-online-checklist-for-college-students-india}

- 🔐 **Use strong, unique passwords** for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols). Avoid using your name or birthdate.

- 📲 **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on apps like Instagram, Gmail, and banking apps for extra security.

- ⚠️ **Do not click on suspicious links** in emails, SMS, or DMs---especially messages claiming prizes, urgent account issues, or job offers.

- 👀 **Verify before you trust**---check official websites or contact sources directly if something feels off.

- 🌐 **Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks** like banking or logging into important accounts.

- 📤 **Limit personal information sharing** on social media (e.g., phone number, address, college ID).

- 💻 **Keep your devices updated** with the latest software and antivirus protection.

- 🎭 **Be cautious of fake profiles and scams**---don't send money or personal info to people you only know online.

### **💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips** {#financial-upi-safety-tips}

- 💸 **Never share your UPI PIN or OTP**---no bank or app (like Google Pay or PhonePe) will ever ask for it.

- 📥 **Be careful with "Collect Request" scams**---you should only approve payments you initiated. Receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN.

### **🚨 Report Cyber Crime** {#report-cyber-crime}

- 🌐 Report online fraud at the [[National Cyber Crime Portal]{.underline}](https://cybercrime.gov.in?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

- 📞 Call **Helpline: 1930** immediately for financial fraud or suspicious transactions
