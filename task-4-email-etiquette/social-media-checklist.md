### <a name="_kv6v7v80ukc1"></a>**📋 Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students**

|**✅ Do’s**|**❌ Don’ts**|
| :-: | :-: |
|Think before you post – ensure it reflects well on you|Don’t overshare personal information (address, phone, etc.)|
|Protect your privacy with strong passwords & settings|Don’t engage in cyberbullying or harmful behavior|
|Verify information before sharing|Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content|
|Be respectful and kind in interactions|Don’t believe everything you see online blindly|
|Use social media for learning and networking|Don’t let social media distract from your studies|
